The 5G Revolutionüåê

5G (Fifth Generation) wireless technology began its commercial rollout around 2019, promising a massive leap over 4G/LTE.

The dots on the map illustrate this early global deployment phase. Green dots indicate Commercial Availability, showing where carriers have officially launched services to consumers.

Key improvements over 4G:

  • Peak Speed: Up to 10 Gbps (100x faster).
  • Latency: As low as 1 millisecond, crucial for autonomous vehicles and remote surgery.
  • Capacity: Ability to connect millions of devices per square kilometer, enabling the massive Internet of Things (IoT).

This map captures the world in a moment of rapid transition, showing which cities and operators were at the forefront of this technological shift.
